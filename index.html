Japchae

This classic Korean noodle dish is made from clear, sweet potato noodles (dangmyeon). These noodles are first tossed in sesame oil, sesame seeds, a little sugar, and soy sauce; it is then, stir-fried with loads of vegetables.

Pho - Vietnam

The dish is made with chicken broth, rice noodles called bánh phở and a host of spices including ginger, star anise, and coriander seeds. Garnished with Thai basil, cilantro, mint, scallions, chillies, fresh lime, and chicken.
